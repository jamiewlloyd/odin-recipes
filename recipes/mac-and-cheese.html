Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description:

This homemade mac and cheese recipe uses a classic mixture of sharp Cheddar and Parmesan. Cheddar is a fantastic base, while Parmesan adds a more complex and salty flavor. Of course, you can adapt this recipe to suit your taste: Gruyere, Monterey Jack, and Gouda are all great options for macaroni and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked macaroni noodles
  • Butter
  • flour
  • whole milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Dried bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  3. Make the cheese sauce.
  4. Pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  5. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese.
  6. Sprinkle with paprika.
  7. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
